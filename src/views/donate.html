Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, Talent Beyond Boundaries is a registered 501(c)(3) organization. No goods or services are received in exchange for your donation. To claim a deduction on your U.S. taxes, please keep your email donation receipt as your official record.
Do I get a receipt?
A donation receipt will be sent to you at the email address you provide. If you select a recurring monthly donation, you will be sent an individual receipt each month after your donation has been processed.
Is my donation secure?
Yes, the security of your information is our highest priority. The application we use employs SSL certificate encryption technologies to ensure transactions are processed in a safe and secure manner. We will not sell, trade, or share your personal information with anyone else, and will only share personal information if you have given us specific permission to do so.
