Talent Beyond Boundaries is the only organization in the world connecting refugees to international job opportunities, opening labor mobility as a complementary solution to traditional refugee resettlement.

Our mission is to open international employment pathways for skilled refugees to fill talent gaps.

War and conflict have forced millions of skilled people to find refuge in places where they are unable to work legally. Talent Beyond Boundaries is working to connect this often excluded talent pool with employers around the world who are searching for skilled workers.

The pathways that Talent Beyond Boundaries is establishing allow refugees to find stability and employers to gain new talent, while reducing the number of dependents on aid.

How We Work​

Link refugees

We work with refugees to provide them with the opportunity and support needed to obtain international employment and rebuild their careers.

Connect employees

We work with companies to find top talent that meets their needs while also giving them the opportunity to contribute to a significant social cause.

Showcase talent

By mapping the hard-earned skills and professional backgrounds of the talent pool, we are highlighting what refugees could contribute if able to reenter the workforce.

Champion labor mobility

Creating pathways for refugees to move for job opportunities benefits the individual and their family, the international community and economy. We are committed to sharing our learnings so others can build upon this critical work.

10,000 Registered Professionals

Talent Beyond Boundaries has registered over 10,000 displaced individuals in its Talent Catalog, an online database that allows refugees to share information about their work experience, professional qualifications, and skills. Talent Beyond Boundaries establishes partnerships with companies around the world who are interested in recruiting from the Talent Catalog and supporting a solution to the refugee crisis.
