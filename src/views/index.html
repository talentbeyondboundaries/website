Talent Beyond Boundaries is the only organisation in the world connecting refugees to international job opportunities, opening labor mobility as a complementary solution to traditional refugee resettlement.

Help Us Create Opportunities

War and conflict have forced many millions of skilled people to find refuge in places where they are unable to work legally. Talent Beyond Boundaries is working to connect this often excluded talent pool with employers around the world who are searching for talented workers.

The complementary pathway for refugees that Talent Beyond Boundaries is establishing allows refugees to find stability, employers to gain new talent, and provides the global economy more active participants, while also reducing the number of dependents on aid.

Learn More Play Video

Our mission is to open international employment pathways for skilled refugees to fill talent gaps.

We achieve this through outreach to refugees throughout Lebanon and Jordan, and in close collaboration with companies, partner organizations, and governments. Talent Beyond Boundaries is the only group connecting this talent pool to employers who are interested in recruiting globally. Our goal is to serve as a catalyst for developing labor mobility for refugees.

Connect employees

Creating pathways for refugees to move for job opportunities is both logical and beneficial to the international community and the global economy. We are sharing our learnings with others who can build upon this critical work.
