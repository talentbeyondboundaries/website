Partner With Us

Our partners recognize the immense economic and social value when a refugee is given access to the global labor market.

Our partners see labor mobility as a key solution and understand that it is time for catalytic change to make it a safe, legal, and practical option for refugees.

Talent Beyond Boundaries’ partners include a close network of passionate individuals, institutions, and corporations who aim to create a paradigm shift in how the world approaches skilled refugees.

If your organization is interested in becoming a partner with TBB, please contact Laura Schrier at lschrier@talentbeyondboundaries.org

Global Partners

Our work is made possible through partnership with a number of dedicated organizations based internationally. TBB has a cooperation agreement with the UN Refugee Agency. In addition, TBB’s implementing partner in Lebanon and Jordan is SPARK, a Dutch NGO with extensive experience in the Middle East and beyond.

Regional Partners

TBB also partners with local and international organizations across Lebanon and Jordan to share information about labor mobility for refugees and TBB’s Talent Catalog. TBB conducts information sessions (including joint sessions with UNHCR), staff briefings, and outreach through its partners, which include:

  • Danish Refugee Council
  • LASER
  • Union of Relief and Development Associations
  • Jesuit Refugee Service
  • Souriyat Across Borders

Corporate Partners

To become a corporate partner of TBB, please visit the For Employers section of our website. We are always looking for corporate partners that can benefit from hiring qualified employees from our talent pool.
